Developer's Note

A quick guide for this prototype.

1

Fill in Your Details

This is a front-end only demo. Use valid information, including a 12-digit number for Aadhaar or an ID like ID12345678 for authorities.

2

Get Your OTP

Click "Send OTP". A notification will appear on the top-right with your simulated 6-digit OTP.

3

Create Account

Enter the OTP from the notification, or simply click the notification to auto-fill it, and create your account.
