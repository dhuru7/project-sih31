Developer's Note

A quick guide for this prototype.

1

Enter a Test ID

This is a front-end only demo. Use a 12-digit number (for citizens) or an ID like ID12345678 (for authorities).

2

Get Your OTP

Click "Send OTP". A notification will appear on the top-right with your simulated 6-digit OTP.

3

Log In

Enter the OTP from the notification, or simply click the notification to auto-fill it.
