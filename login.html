Developer Note & Simulation Guide

Please Note: This login page is a front-end demonstration and is not connected to a live backend system.

How the Simulation Works:

  1. ID Number: Enter any valid 12-digit number for your Aadhaar or a valid Authority ID that you used to sign up.
  2. OTP Generation: Clicking 'Send OTP' will simulate a request and automatically download a PNG image containing your unique 6-digit OTP.
  3. Verification: Open the downloaded image and enter the OTP provided in the boxes to complete the login process.
